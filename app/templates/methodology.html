Ethereum Gross Decentralised Product (GDP) Methodology

Project Overview

The ETH GDP Calculator aims to measure Ethereum's economic activity through a comprehensive framework inspired by traditional GDP calculations but adapted for decentralized economies. Unlike traditional companies, blockchain networks like Ethereum represent emerging digital economies with their own monetary systems, financial infrastructure, and cultural outputs.

Decentralised GDP

GDPdecentralised = M + K + F + S + P + C
  • M: Monetary Base
  • K: Capital Stock (TVL)
  • F: Fee Revenue
  • S: Stablecoins
  • P: Protocol Value
  • C: Cultural Capital

Traditional GDP

GDPtraditional = C + I + G + NX
  • C: Consumption
  • I: Investment
  • G: Government Spending
  • NX: Net Exports

Why This Matters

  • Monetary system and policy
  • Financial infrastructure
  • Cultural outputs
  • Economic activity metrics
